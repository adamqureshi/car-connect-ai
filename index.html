What CarConnect AI does

Not another marketplace. A direct inventory connection layer for AI shopping.

Dealers already pay for listings. CarConnect AI is different: it helps your store show up inside AI-assisted shopping with fresher data and direct paths back to your dealership.

Connect the feed

We work with your DMS or inventory provider to pull inventory, pricing, photos, and VDP links into a structured format.

Normalize the data

We clean up store info, vehicle data, and contact points so AI experiences can read your inventory clearly and present it correctly.

Launch the channel

We publish an AI-ready shopping layer that helps shoppers discover vehicles and land on your site, your lead forms, and your phone numbers.
